ABOUT ME

I'm motivated by the experiences I've had throughout my life and I want to share those experiences through the content I create for you. Experience

Photography

I focus on portrait photography. Faces have always been an interest to me for no particular reason other than wanting to translate myself better as a person. I will also use faces in my digital art where I compose portraits with other visual elements. I currently shoot with a Nikon Z6 Camera and work with the parts of the full adobe suite to complete my work.

Digital Art

The Digital Art I create is a combination of my portrait photography and visual content I Get from sources like Adobe Stock and other sources of Royalty-Free content. I make this work for more emotive purposes and use it to motivate other creators. My digital compositions are very impulsive and therapeutic to make. That's what I hope comes across.

Motion Design

Motion Design is the process of rendering movement More than that it is the process of creating a specific and intentional Mood or feeling. More than that it is the process of using Various techniques to create a mood. I use that to present my work.

Animation

Animation is an art used to create physical movement Digitally or literally. I often work digitally and present My animations through my videography. My animation Style is contemporary and often represent more More methods of animation via. Digital work.
